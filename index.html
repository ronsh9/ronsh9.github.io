I'm a senior at MIT studying mathematics and computer science. My goal is to develop world models that can understand the physical world beyond human-level intelligence in a scalable and principled manner. Ultimately, I aim to use these world models to augment our capabilities across many disciplines. This involves deploying agents that can:
Learn from unlabeled high-bandwith signals.
Reason about and interact with their environment.
Discover or process new information that's observed dynamically.
