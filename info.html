This website is inspired by the website of David August. If you haven't heard of him before, you should listen to this. It's one of the most unique electronic music performances I've ever heard. I decided to adapt it into a personal website as it reminded me of the structure of coding IDEs as well as some computational chemistry software such as GROMACS. Other than that, it was my intention to create a minimalistic website to emphasize the content over the visualizations. While I was strongly tempted to do it the ol' school way with raw html, I eventually decided to go for something a bit more sophisticated. While this argument isn't very well motivated, you may read more about website design here. The Potpourri section is inspired by Michael Albergo's website.
